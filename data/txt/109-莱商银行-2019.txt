置力度。不断完善存量风险资产处置化解工作机制，强化不良资产台账式管理，严格落实风险处置计划与措施，加快存量风险处置化解步伐。（二）市场风险及对策市场风险是指因市场价格（利率、汇率、股票价格和商品价格）的不利变动，从而使银行表内外业务发生损失的风险。报告期内，公司从以下方面加强市场风险管理：一是强化指标监测预警。逐日监控市场风险限额执行及市场信用违约情况，按季监测利率敏感性指标，及时采取措施消除风险隐患。二是加大资金业务风险管控。根据全行资产负债规划，合理配置投资资产种类及期限结构，提升短期资金利用率及收益水平，规避市场波动带来的利率风险。三是加强银行账簿利率风险管理。依托资产负债管理系统，建设完成银行账簿利率风险的计量体系，实现银行账簿利率风险计量的独立审查和验证。同时定期开展银行账户利率风险压力测试，根据压力测试结果，适时适当调整优化未来利率期限结构。四是规范外汇业务流程管控。科学制定外汇业务操作规程，推行外汇资金交易分级授权管理，强化资金交易员权限限制。同时严格执行代客结售汇业务平盘制度，对代客大额结售汇业务逐笔平盘，降低汇率波动带来的汇率损失。(三)操作风险及对策操作风险是指由不完善或有问题的内部控制程序、员工、信息科技系统以及外部事件所造成损失的风险。报告期内，公司从以下方面加强操作风险管理：一是组织开展各类监督检查。制定年度合规检查与风险排查计划35项，组织开展全行性业务监督检查工作，全面梳理排查操作风险隐患，完善11各项制度流程，加大问题整改和问责力度，有效规范员工操作行为，保障各项业务合规有序开展。二是组织开展“巩固治乱象成果”“行业规范建设年”工作，对照监管要求，对历次内外部检查、深化整治乱象、飞行检查等发现的问题进行“回头看”检查，对道德规范、行为规范、服务规范、管理规范等进行梳理总结和补充。三是持续增强员工行为管理。定期开展员工行为排查，及时排除风险隐患。同时组织开展案防合规知识学习测试，组织全员分批次参加考核，进一步提升全员自律意识、案防意识、合规意识。四是全力构筑法律风险防线。以持续强化法律服务支撑为基础，扎实推进格式合同电子化、非格式合同检查、法律知识培训等工作，建立健全内外部律师管理制度流程，严格防范法律风险。(四)流动性风险及对策流动性风险是指公司无法以合理成本及时获得充足资金，用于偿付到期债务、履行其他支付义务和满足正常业务开展的其他资金需求的风险。报告期内，公司从以下方面加强流动性风险管理：一是强化流动性监测预测。加强资金缺口分析预测，做好流动性指标动态计量与监测，全面提升流动性风险识别及预警效率，有效防范流动性风险。二是优化资产负债期限结构。负债端，不断加大存款营销力度，持续推动存款快速增长，降低同业融资比例；合理设置FTP定价策略，引导分支机构加大营销中长期存款积极性。资产端，加大资产到期期限管理，合理确定新增贷款到期期限分布，适当控制长期投资业务，促进月度资金流入均衡平稳，有效降低资产负债期限错配。三是增持优质流动性资产。通过减持企业债、政策性金融债和增持国债等措施，扩大优质流动性资产储备规模，保障优质流动性资产充足率持续达标。四是强化风险应对能力。组织开展流动性压力测试，合理设置测试情景，充分揭示潜在流动性风险，进一步修订完善流动性风险管控措施。组织开展流动性应急演练，重点模拟挤兑等极端情形下，总分支机构联动处置能力，进一步提升流动性风险应对能力。（五）信息科技风险及对策信息科技风险是指信息科技在银行运用过程中，由于自然因素、人为因素、技术漏洞和管理缺陷产生的操作、法律和声誉等风险。报告期内，公司从以下方面加强信息科技风险管理：一是完善信息科技治理体系。组织召开信息科技管理委员会会议，审议通过2019年度信息科技预算等18项议案，修订完善《莱商银行信息系统安全管理办法》等44项制度，努力构建信息科技风险防范长效治理机制。二是推进信息科技基础设施建设。不断加大基础设施投入，顺利完成UPS功率模块扩容、域名解析服务器部署、数据中心机房改造等工作，并建成启用总行生产运维操作室，信息科技风控基础不断夯实。三是增强网络安全管理能力。扎实开展内网安全管理系统升级、网络防火墙体系建设等工作，分步骤对全行无线设备进行风险排查和问题整改，信息网络安全水平显著提升。四是强化信息安全保护。顺利完成六期基础软硬件产品缺陷信息协查、重要信息系统等保定级、数据库审计系统升级等工12作，信息安全保护能力逐步提升。五是提升业务连续性管理能力。在做好灾备机房、UPS设备日常巡检的基础上，组织开展业务系统切换演练、消防系统演练等22次，启动同城灾备中心建设，并上线运行数据备份系统，业务连续性管理能力进一步增强。十、内部控制评价公司高度重视内部控制建设，遵循全覆盖、制衡性、审慎性及相匹配的内控原则，建立健全内部控制管理体系，不断深化内控管理体制机制改革，持续完善内控管理制度流程，全面贯彻落实各项内控管理措施。通过对内部控制体系建设、实施和运行结果开展系统性调查、测试、分析和评估，公司内部控制体系从内部控制环境、风险识别与评估、内部控制措施与执行、内部检查监督、信息交流与反馈等方面，基本涵盖了所有业务、产品、渠道、管理及其关键环节等内控要素，内部控制体系基本健全，流程设置科学合理，在强化公司治理、推动转型发展、创新业务产品、防范化解风险等方面得到了有效的执行。十一、内部审计情况公司设立内审部为内部审计机构，负责对公司所有的业务和经营管理活动、经营状况进行综合审计评价。内审部对董事会及其审计委员会负责，内审工作由监事长分管，内审部分别向董事会和监事会报告审计结果，审计工作具有独立性。报告期内，公司围绕发展战略和中心任务，严格落实行业监管要求，坚持以问题和风险为导向，以防案控险为目标，合理拟订年度审计计划，紧盯重点机构、重点领域、重点岗位和重要环节，组织实施对全行各项业务活动、经营情况及重要岗位人员任期经济责任的审计，监督、评价法律、法规和规章制度的执行情况，向管理层提出改进建议，督促、跟进整改落实，积极发挥服务监督职能，推动全行制度、流程、系统持续完善，保障业务合规、稳健发展。13Chapter The company has established a scientific remuneration management structure, clearly stipulating that 
the remuneration of directors and supervisors shall be decided by the general meeting of shareholders, 
and the Board of Directors shall decide the remuneration of senior managers. The Board of Directors 
is ultimately responsible for the salary management of the Bank, responsible for reviewing, approving, 
revising the remuneration management system and policies, and remuneration cost and budget of the 
Bank, supervising the implementation of the Bank's remuneration system, and regularly assessing 
the soundness and effectiveness of the remuneration mechanism. Under the Board of Directors is 
the Nomination and Remuneration Assessment Committee, which is responsible for reviewing work 
relating to the remuneration system, policy and supervision. Senior management is responsible for the 
implementation of resolutions of the Board of Directors about the remuneration management, organizing 
to develop and update the remuneration management system and policies, managing and controlling 
salary budget and cost, reviewing and approving the employees' wage fixing and salary increase plan, 
performance appraisal plan and staff benefit plan, etc. (The remuneration of the senior managers of the 
Company incorporated in the scope of the heads of state-owned enterprises in Jinan shall be determined

The remuneration system of the Company conforms to the payment concept of the "market-oriented, 
post-oriented, performance-oriented, capability-oriented" principle. In 2016, the Company carried out the 
optimization and design program for the remuneration system, implemented a fixed remuneration system 
on a monthly basis according to the staff's post responsibilities and standards of person-to-job matching, 
linked the total remuneration to the operating profit throughout the Bank, established a top-down 
withdrawal mechanism with total remuneration linked to the operating profit as well as bottom-up total 
remuneration accounting mechanism, and gradually improved the management and control mechanism

照61Chapter V Corporate Governance StructureI. Corporate governance of the CompanyDuring the reporting period, the Company strictly observed the related provisions in the 
Company Law and the Law on Commercial Banks, conscientiously implemented the related 
regulations of the regulatory intuition on corporate governance, and was dedicated to further 
improving corporate governance structure, enhancing the effectiveness of the corporate governance 
mechanism and making the decision of the Board of Directors more scientific. The performance of 
the Company in its corporate governance is as follows:(i) Shareholders and shareholders' general meetingThe Company convened the shareholders' general meeting in accordance with the requirement 
of the Articles of Association and the Rules of Procedures for Shareholders' Meeting and actively 
listened to shareholders' opinion and suggestion to ensure that all shareholders have the right to 
know, to participate and to vote to the major events of the Company.During the reporting period, the 
Company held its 2018 annual shareholders' general meeting, and 2019 first interim shareholders' 
general meeting. The meetings went through related legal procedures in strict accordance with the 
related regulations to ensure that shareholders participate in the meetings and exercise their right. 
Also, legal opinion was issued by a lawyer.(ii) Directors and the Board of DirectorsCurrently, the Company has 9 directors, including 3 independent directors. The Board of 
Directors is able to conscientiously perform its duties in strict accordance with the provisions in the 
Articles of Association, make decisions with the authorization of the shareholders' general meeting, 
strengthen the corporate governance, advance strategic steps and promote steady operation which 
safeguarded the interests of the Company and all shareholders. During the reporting period, the 
Company held 8 board meetings totally.Currently, under the Board of Directors of the Company there is 1 office and 6 special 
committees, including the Office of Board of Directors, Related Transaction Control Committee, 
Risk Management Committee, Strategic Development Committee, Audit Committee, Nomination 
and Remuneration Assessment Committee and Consumer Rights Protection Committee. Under 
the Risk Management Committee are the Special Committee of Reputation Risk Management and 
Special Committee of IT Risk Management.These 5 special committees have specific scopes of 
power, supervise and manage the matters at different aspects, and are accountable to the Board of 
Directors. They provide constructive suggestions for the decision making of the Board of Directors 
further to improve the science of the decisions made by the Board of Directors.(iii) Supervisors and the Board of SupervisorsThe Company has 9 supervisors, including 3 employee supervisors and 3 external supervisors.
The Board of Supervisors conscientiously performs its duties in strict accordance with the62provisions in the Articles of Association, carries out supervision and inspection over the compliance 
of the Company's finance, risk management, asset quality and the duty performance of the senior 
management, communicates with the external audit units and management regularly or irregularly, 
conducts targeted special inspections, and puts forward suggestions and improvement opinions to 
the Board of Directors and the management according to hidden risks or problems discovered, so as 
to play the role of the Board of Supervisors more effectively.(iv) Information disclosure and transparencyThe Company published its 2018 Annual Report on Financial Times and its website, and 
prepared a separate edition of 2018 Annual Report of Laishang Co., Ltd. (Summary), to carry out 
information disclosure in a timely and accurate manner and safeguard the interests of shareholders, 
particularly medium and small shareholders.II. Independent Directors and Their Performance of DutiesDuring the reporting period, there were 3 independent directors on the Board of Directors. 
With an attitude of being responsible to all shareholders, these independent directors performed the 
obligation of integrity and diligence, earnestly participated in the board meetings and meetings of 
various special committees, and actively delivered their opinions to safeguard the collective interest 
of the Company and the legitimate rights and interest of medium and small shareholders.(i) Independent directors' presence in meetingsDuring the reporting period, the number of independent directors attending the board meetings 
should be 17, with presence in 15 persons, entrusted attendance in 2 persons, and absence in 0 
person.(ii) Objections of the independent directors to related affairs of the CompanyDuring the reporting period, the independent directors of the Company did not object to anyproposal of the Board of Directors of the current year.III. year.IV. Operation and decision-making system of the CompanyThe highest authority of the Company is the Shareholders' General Meeting, which is managed

employed by the Board of Directors, is responsible for all daily operation and management of 
the Company. Only the head office of the Company is its legal person, and all branches and sub-
branches are non-independent accounting units, whose operation and management activities shall be 
carried out with the authorization of the Company and who shall be responsible for the Company.The Company has no controlling shareholder. The Company and its majority shareholders
