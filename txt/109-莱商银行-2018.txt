行为，进一步增强全员红线意识。五是认真落实案件防控工作要求。印发年度案防工作指导意见，明确案防工作重点领域和关键环节，扎实开展交流轮岗、离任审计、强制休假等工作。(四)流动性风险及对策流动性风险是指本行无法以合理成本及时获得充足资金，用于偿付到期债务、履行其他支付义务和满足正常业务开展的其他资金需求的风险。报告期内，公司从以下方面加强流动性风险管理：一是完善流动性风险管理制度体系。梳理、诊断公司流动性风险管控流程，修订完善流动性风险管理办法等制度，设定合理的流动性风险限额并强化监测报告，确保流动性需求持续得到保障。二是强化流动性指标预警分析。加大备付金比率、流动性比例、流动性缺口率等指标监测分析，及时识别流动性风险隐患，动态计量流动性风险，并及时采取风险缓释措施，有效防范流动性风险。三是丰富流动性风险管理工具。优化升级资产负债管理系统，进一步提升流动性风险的识别、计量和监测能力，完善现金流模型和流动性压力测试方案,科学预测未来的现金流。四是调整优化负债结构。加强主动负债管理，积极拓宽资金来源，对全行流动性形成有力支撑。同时，通过调整FTP定价策略，引导分支机构积极拓展稳定性负债，促使负债稳定性进一步提升。五是定期开展压力测试。通过开展压力测试，测算不同压力情景下流动性管理可能面临的风险因素，并对流动性应急预案进行修正，确保流动性触发应急预案时风险能够得到有效处置和控制。（五）信息科技风险及对策0信息科技风险是指信息科技在银行运用过程中，由于自然因素、人为因素、技术漏洞和管理缺陷产生的操作、法律和声誉等风险。报告期内，公司从以下方面加强信息科技风险管理：一是加大信息科技治理力度。组织召开三次信息科技管理委员会会议，修订完善信息系统突发事件应急管理办法等制度29项，健全信息科技风险管理流程框架。二是开展信息科技风险排查。通过印发信息安全风险提示、开展基础软硬件产品缺陷信息协查等工作，全面排查信息科技风险隐患，并进行系统性整改落实，进一步提升信息科技风险管控能力。三是强化网络风险监测。部署总行互联网行为审计系统和分支行互联网认证审计系统，实现互联网的微信认证和审计。组织无线网络安全管理专项自查和风险评估，开展无线网络渗透测试，提升公司物理网络安全。四是加强信息科技业务连续性管理。组织开展数据中心机房市电整体切换演练、同城清算系统突发事件应急演练等各类应急演练40余次，进一步提升各类业务连续性。五是增强客户信息保护力度。建立客户信息保护工作台账，梳理生产系统敏感信息，完成批量导出报表敏感字段屏蔽工作，建设企业网盘，强化客户信息保护。八、内部控制制度的完整性、合理性与有效性公司高度重视内控制度建设，秉承全面、审慎、有效原则，不断健全内控管理体系，确保各类内控制度流程科学、严密，保障公司高质量稳健发展。报告期内，公司制定深化制度建设工作方案，在全行范围内组织开展制度宣讲培训及竞赛工作，增强全员对制度的敬畏意识和执行能力。同时，根据国家政策和公司发展需要不断改进和完善内控制度体系，将合规管理要求及规章制度映射到各项经营活动、各业务条线、各岗位员工。报告期内，公司制度评审委员会组织召开评审会议11次，审议新建制度38项。通过对内部控制体系建设、实施和运行结果开展系统性调查、测试、分析和评估，公司内部制度体系基本涵盖了全行所有产品、渠道、交易、管理及其关键环节，流程设置科学合理，在规范财务会计行为、提高会计信息质量、强化经营管理、控制经营风险、堵塞漏洞、防止舞弊等方面得到了有效的执行。Chapter Risks of the Company and risk managementThe comprehensive risk management system of the Company consists of the Board of Directors, the 
Board of Supervisors, the senior management, the comprehensive risk management department, the main 
control department for risk management, the responsible department for risk management, the internal 
audit department, and all Branches. The Board of Directors is responsible for establishing and maintaining 
an effective and comprehensive risk management system and ultimately for risk management. The 
senior management is responsible for formulating the risk management policies and procedures and for 
implementing comprehensive risk management. The senior management has set up special committees 
such as the Credit Risk Review Committee and the Assets-Liabilities Management Committee, and also 
designated the main control departments and management departments of various risks. In 2018, the 
Company always stuck to the operating concept of legal compliance, followed the principle of "matching 
risks and returns", constantly improved the comprehensive risk management system, continuously 
optimized the risk management systems, made solid efforts to promote the implementation of risk 
management measures, and spread risk management to cover all business and operational links, so that 
the comprehensive risk management level has been significantly improved. During the reporting period, 
the main risks faced by the Company include credit risk, market risk, operational risk, liquidity risk, IT 
risk, and etc.(i) Credit risks and their countermeasures
Credit risk refers to the risks of economic losses to the Company caused by the influence on the value 
of financial products resulted from failure to fulfil the obligations stipulated in the contract of or changes 
of the credit quality of debtors or counterparties. During the reporting period, the Company strengthened 
the credit risk management from the following aspects. Firstly, the Company enhanced the guiding role of 
the differentiated policies, by defining the annual guidelines of the credit granting policies, establishing 
and perfecting client sand tables for old and new capacity conversion, green industries and so on, and 
realizing accurate release of credit resources. Secondly, the Company optimized the review and approval 
procedures. By improving the survey report template for credit rating and credit extension, implementing 
the review and closing within a certain time limit for credit extension, and other measures, the Company 
strictly controlled the access to credit, strengthened the early warning management for credit granting, 
and effectively improved the quality and efficiency of credit approval. Thirdly, the Company standardized 
the management of credit payment. A central mode for loan payment was fully implemented so that all 
credits were released by the relatively independent loan center after examination and review, and the 
conditions of credit granting were strengthened to implement. Fourthly, the Company strengthened the 
post-evaluation of credit granting business. Starting from the perspectives of the implementation of the 
credit granting policies, examination and approval of credit granting, quality of credit granting data, and 
etc., the Company comprehensively carried out the post-evaluation of credit granting business, urged the 
units which were found with problems to conduct investigation and rectification in a timely manner, so 
as to overall improve the quality of credit granting business. Fifthly, the Company deepened the efforts 
to improve the asset quality, convening a thematic promotion meeting, implementing the responsibility 
system for the disposal of non-performing credit assets, setting up the full-time team for settlement and 
collection, and preparing the schedules for the disposal of non-accrued loans one by one, so as to promote 
the quality and efficiency of the settlement and collection of non-performing assets.
      (ii)  Market risks and their countermeasuresMarket risk refers to the risks of losses of in-balance-sheet and off-balance-sheet businesses due to 
adverse changes in market price (interest rate, exchange rate, stock price and commodity price). During 
the reporting period, the Company strengthened the market risk management from the following aspects. 
Firstly, the Company perfected the market risk management system by establishing the market risk 
management system which matches with the nature, scale and complexity of business, making clear the 
responsibilities of "two boards and one level (the Board of Directors, the Board of Supervisors, and themanagement)", and of main control and responsible departments of market risks, formulating the clear 
market risk management policies and procedures, and optimizing the tools and methods to recognize, 
measure, monitor and evaluate market risks. Secondly, the Company strengthened the monitoring and 
analysis of market risks by establishing and improving the market risk reporting system, monitoring the 
implementation of market risk limits and credit risk defaults on a daily basis, and regularly reporting 
the market risk management status to the senior management and relevant departments. At the same 
time, the limit index operation was monitored on a daily basis, risk pre-warning was carried out timely 
according to the monitoring results, and relevant departments were asked to develop solutions. Thirdly, 
the Company strengthened the limit management by formulating a risk limit scheme for bond business, 
clearly specifying trading limit, risk limit and loss-stopping limit for investment business, and effectively 
standardizing the bond investment business. Fourthly, the Company optimized the investment strategy and 
asset allocation structure, by actively adjusting the investment strategies for financial market businesses, 
appropriately reducing the scale of risky assets, and effectively eliminating the impact of market risks. 
Fifthly, the Company strengthened the foundation for managing foreign exchange businesses, by 
formulating the rules and regulations such as the Management Measures for Foreign Exchange Funds, 
clarifying the daily operation authority of fund traders, and strengthening the trading discipline and limit 
control.(iii) Operational risks and their countermeasures 
Operational risk refers to the risks of loss caused by imperfect or problematic internal control 
procedures, employees, IT systems, or external events. During the reporting period, the Company 
strengthened the operational risk management from the following aspects. Firstly, the Company deeply 
implemented the various works of the "Year of Basic Management Improvement", by setting 2018 as the 
"Year of Basic Management Improvement", formulating the implementation plan and work allocation 
table, focusing on solving the weak links of basic management during the development, further improving 
the efficiency, strengthening the internal control, and preventing risks, so as to achieve the compliance 
and efficient development of various businesses. Secondly, the Company organized and carried out the 
compliance inspections and risk investigation and screening, by formulating the annual special risk 
investigation and screening plan, organizing and carrying out the rectification and control of market 
chaos and inspection of bill businesses within the whole Bank, carrying out systematic rectification from 
the aspects of management, system and execution based on all the problems found in the inspections, 
so as to further fill up the gaps in management and execution and prevent potential risks and hidden 
dangers. Thirdly, the Company established and put online the internal control compliance and operational 
risk management platform, and accordingly sorting the business management process, risk points, 
control measures and so on to realize the effective integration of the resources for internal control & 
compliance and operational risk management. Fourthly, the Company strengthened the management of 
employee behavior, by continuously inspecting and screening employee behavior, overall investing and 
screening the abnormal behavior of employees such as participation in private lending through credit 
investigation and screening, credit publicity, home visits and other means, so as to further enhance the red 
line awareness of all staff. Fifthly, the Company earnestly implemented the work requirements of case 
prevention and control, by issuing the annual guidelines on case prevention, clearly identifying important 
areas and key links in the work of case prevention, and carrying out exchanges, job rotation, off-office 
auditing, and mandatory vacations in a solid manner.
(iv) Liquidity risks and their countermeasuresLiquidity risk refers to the risks of inability to timely obtain sufficient funds at a reasonable cost 
for the purpose of repaying due debts, fulfilling other payment obligations or meeting other needs for 
funds used for normal business. During the reporting period, the Company strengthened the liquidity risk 
management from the following aspects. Firstly, the Company improved the liquidity risk management 
system, by sorting out and diagnosing the liquidity risk management and control process, revising and 
improving the liquidity risk management methods and other systems, setting reasonable liquidity risk 
limits and strengthening monitoring reports, so as to ensure that liquidity demands are continuously 
guaranteed. Secondly, the Company strengthened the early warning analysis of liquidity indexes, byincreasing the monitoring and analysis of reserve ratio, liquidity ratio, liquidity gap ratio and other 
indexes, timely identifying potential liquidity risks, dynamically measuring liquidity risks, and taking 
timely risk mitigation measures, so as to effectively prevent liquidity risks. Thirdly, the Company enriched 
the liquidity risk management tools, by optimizing and upgrading the management system of assets 
and liabilities, further improving the ability to identify, measure and monitor liquidity risks, perfecting 
the cash flow model and testing scheme of liquidity stress, and scientifically predicting the future cash 
flow. Fourthly, the Company adjusted and optimized the debt structure, by strengthening active liability 
management and expanding sources of funds, so as to provide strong support for the liquidity of the 
whole Bank. At the same time, the Company guided the Branches to actively expand the stable debts 
by adjusting the FTP pricing strategy, so as to promote the stability of liabilities. Fifthly, the Company 
carried out regular stress tests so as to measure and calculate the risk factors that may be faced by liquidity 
management under different stress scenarios, and modified the liquidity emergency plan so as to ensure 
that risks can be effectively handled and controlled when the liquidity emergency plan is triggered.
(v)  IT risks and their countermeasuresInformation technology (IT) risk refers to the operational, legal and reputation risks generated due 
to natural factors, human factors, technical loopholes and management defects in the application process 
of information technology in banks. During the reporting period, the Company strengthened the IT risk 
management from the following aspects. Firstly, the Company intensified the governance of information 
technology by organizing and holding three meetings of the IT Management Committee, revising and 
improving 29 systems such as the Management Measures for Emergency Events of Information System, 
and improving the process and framework for managing IT risks. Secondly, the Company carried out 
the investigation and screening of IT risks, by comprehensively inspecting and screening out IT-related 
risks and hidden dangers through printing and issuing information security risk tips and carrying out 
information-assisted investigation of the defects of basic software and hardware products, and conducting 
systematic rectification and implementation, so as to further improve the management and control ability 
for IT risks. Thirdly, the Company strengthened the network risk monitoring by deploying the Internet 
behavior audit system of the Head Office and the Internet certification audit system of the Branches to 
realize the WeChat certification and audit based on the Internet; organizing special self-inspection and 
risk assessment for the wireless network security management, and carrying out the penetration test of 
the wireless network, so as to improve the Company's physical network security. Fourthly, the Company 
strengthened the continuity management of IT businesses, by organizing and carrying out more than 
40 times of various emergency drills, such as the overall switching exercise of the power supply in the 
computer room of the Data Center, and the emergency drill of the citywide clearing system, so as to 
further improve the continuity of various businesses. Fifthly, the Company strengthened the protection 
of customer information, by setting up the work log for customer information protection, sorting out 
sensitive information of the production system, completing the shielding work of sensitive fields in batch 
exported reports and forms, and building the enterprise network disk, so as to strengthen the customer 
information protection.

The company has established a scientific remuneration management structure, clearly stipulating that 
the remuneration of directors and supervisors shall be decided by the general meeting of shareholders, 
and the Board of Directors shall decide the remuneration of senior managers; the Board of Directors is 
ultimately responsible for the salary management of the Bank, responsible for reviewing, approving, 
revising the compensation management system and policies, and remuneration cost and budget of the 
Bank, supervising the implementation of the Bank’s remuneration system, and regularly assessing 
the soundness and effectiveness of the remuneration mechanism; under the Board of Directors is the 
Nomination and Remuneration Assessment Committee, which is responsible for reviewing work 
relating to the remuneration system, policy and supervision; senior management is responsible for the 
implementation of resolutions of the Board of Directors about the remuneration management, organizing 
to develop and update the remuneration management system and policies, managing and controlling 
salary budget and cost, reviewing and approving the employees' wage fixing and salary increase plan, 
performance appraisal plan and staff benefit plan, etc.2. Total Annual Compensation and Structure Distribution
In 2018, the company’s total compensation was RMB531.81 million. The structure distribution of 
the compensation is as below: RMB184.60 million of fixed compensation, accounting for 34.71% of the 
total amount; RMB191.27 million of variable compensation, accounting for 35.97% of the total amount; 
RMB155.94 million of welfare income such as Five Insurances & Two Funds etc., accounting for 29.32% 
of the total amount.3. The standard of remunerations and performance measurement and risk adjustment
Performance salary of the department / office employees of the Company’s head office shall be paid 
based on appraisal according to the provisions of the Management Measures for Performance Appraisal 
of Department /Office of Laishang Bank’s Head Office, and that of branch employees based on the 
provisions of the Management Measures for Performance Appraisal of Laishang Bank.4. Remuneration system
Guided by the best practices of market, the Company established the salary payment concept of 
"market-oriented, position-oriented, performance-oriented, and ability-oriented". In 2016 the Company 
carried out the design project of optimizing the remuneration system, by person-post matching based 
on job responsibilities and standards of employees, implementing the fixed remuneration system, i.e., 
determining salary based on post and paying  salary on a monthly basis, linking the total remuneration 
to the operating profit of the whole Bank, establishing the top-down extraction mechanism of linking the 
total remuneration to operating profit and the down-top calculation mechanism of the total remuneration, 
so as to gradually improve the management and control mechanism of the total compensation in the whole 
Bank.The Company clearly defined the related provisions for deferred payment of management personnel’
s performance salary, so as to link their performance salary to job responsibilities, and risk prevention 
and control according to the different levels and positions of management personnel, and pay a certain 
proportion of performance salary in a deferred manner.5. Preparation of annual remunerations plan and completion of assessment
The Company’s annual remuneration plan was prepared according to the size of staff, business 
development, and etc. In 2018, various operational indicators of the Company were good, and the 
remunerations reasonably reflected the performance and risk management of the Company.6. Exceptions beyond the original remunerations plan
There was no exception beyond the original remunerations plan in the Company in the reportingperiod.II. 充分发挥战略决策职能，全力推动转型发展一是保持战略定力，加快转型发展。0年，董事会着眼长远、立足当前，积极适应金融发展新形势，高度重视金融科技应用，坚定支持高级管理层深入推进经营转型，将资产负债结构调整作为提升企业效益的总抓手，加大资产流转速度，优化业务结构，着力提高风险资产收益率。0年，全行收入结构进一步优化，营收稳定性进一步增强，在保持对公业务优势的同时，零售业务的价值贡献度持续提升。二是积极推进对标达标与精细化管理。扎实推进基础管理水平提升，进一步理顺条线管理体制，细化工作要求和标准，按照“四环节工作法”的要求，落实具体责任人和工作步骤，明确界定各项工作责任，制定切实工作计划，扎实开展对标达标与精细化管理工作。通过与选定的标杆进行全面对标，研究与标杆单位在管理方式、业务流程和经营指标方面的差距，对比分析差距原因，结合自身实际，制定得力措施，提升全行基础管理水平。三是进一步理顺管理机制，提高服务支撑能力。稳步推进分行组织架构调整，明晰总分行条线管理架构。坚持原则性与灵活性相结合，对分行部室进行合理设置，管理条线更加清晰，部室及岗位职责边界更加明确，向一线、基层倾斜的用人导向更加凸显。四是持续加强对外投资的管理。协助河南方城凤裕村镇银行、天津武清村镇银行拟定更换董事、董事长的相关议案和流程；对四家村镇银行0年度董事会工作报告、经营管理报告、利润分配方案等议案进行了重点审议，并对四家村镇银行进行调研形成报告；针对东营莱商村镇银行、五台莱商村镇银行经营管理、资产质量、风险管控等相关情况进行了实地调研，并对村镇银行下一步发展提出了意见和建议。（三）强化全面风险管理和内部控制，基础管理能力稳步提升一是深入开展资产质量攻坚。不断拓宽不良资产处置渠道，积极争取地方政府及大型优质企业的支持，通过担保代偿、以物抵债、法律诉讼、风险代理等多种方式全力化解不良资产。二是全面落实不良贷款责任认定工作。严格遵循“以事实为依据，抓住关键节点、重要环节，区别关键少数，责权匹配，尽职免责”原则，细化了“责任认定、新老划断、重在当下”三原则的标准和方法，坚持还原场景、细分责任、精准问责，认定过程中对未形成新的不良或未扩大损失的尽职行为予以免责，积极、有效地引导全行不良贷款处置工作。三是完善全面风险管理体系。完善风险管理政策体系，制定《莱商银行全面风险管理指导意见》《莱商银行风险偏好管理办法》等制度，进一步明确全面风险管理部门、风险管理主控部门及责任部门的职责。上线运行操作风险管理系统，涵盖合规管理、操作风险管理、内部控制评价等个模块项功能，制定《莱商银行0年深化制度建设工作方案》，组织开展制度宣讲次，有效调动全员学习制度、执行制度的积极性、主动性。四是全面推进深化整治市场乱象工作。制定《莱商银行进一步深化整治银行业市场乱象工作实施方案》，编印《银监会专项治理文件汇编》，完成整治乱象自查评估及阶段性“上查下”工作，完成整治乱象年度工作报告。针对检查发现问题，完善制度和管理流程，督导分支机构扎实开展问责与整改工作，切实做到系统性整改。（四）健全资本管理和约束机制，夯实经营发展的资本基础一是强化资本节约理念。0年末完成增资扩股后，全行的资本水平虽然得到了有效提升，但由于资本的稀缺性，资本管理的重要性凸显，为此强化了对风险资本回报率的考核，加大了对风险加权资产占用率的考核比重，引导分支机构加快资产结构调整，积极发展轻资本业务，降低风险资本消耗速度。二是积极组织开展内部资本充足评估和压力测试。组织开展了年度内部资本充足评估和压力测试工作，全面评估本行面临的各类实质性风险，及其对本行资本充足状况的影响，为全行内部资本补充提供决策依据，为全行资本使用节约提供了规范标准。三是积极探索新的资本补充渠道。根据《关于进一步支持商业银行资本工具创新的意见》，对目前无固定期限资本债券、转股型二级资本债券等新型资本工具进行了调研，结合全行对未来三年资本缺口的测算，初步拟定了未来资本补充的路径，拟于0年启动资本补充，为全行各项业务发展提供资本支撑。（五）积极履行社会责任，着力提升品牌价值一是全力做好对外宣传。加强与新闻媒体的交流合作，加大正面宣传报道力度，年内向莱芜银行业协会、银监局、市政府信息科、市人行、金融办等报送信息0余条次，在《莱芜日报》金融周刊中发布信息0余篇，正向引导社会舆论，提升了莱商银行社会形象。同时，在莱芜电视台《赢在金融》栏目，进行全行形象和业务产品宣传，取得了较好效果。二是深入开展消费者权益保护工作。0年，全行以监管部门的消费者权益保护工作考核评价办法为基准，继续做好消费者权益保护各项工作，把消保工作深化到与消费者相关的各领域、各条线、各业务范畴，深化到各分支机构。持续做好消费者权益保护制度体系完善工作，优化产品与服务管理机制，通过制度的修订完善明确消费者权益保护内容，进一步丰富产品与服务风险披露的内容和形式，落实产品销售透明性。三是积极参与各类慈善公益事业。积极参与“春蕾计划”“希望工程”、慈善募捐等活动，竭力倡导奉献精神和社会责任意识，0年莱芜地区捐助春蕾儿童人，捐款金额万余元。与市总工会、区总工会联合，在莱芜辖内家网点建立爱心驿站，为环卫工人、农民工等社会群体提供了休息场所。各级工会扎根当地、服务当地，利用端午、中秋、重阳等传统节日，组织员工到敬老院、儿童福利院、孤寡老人、困难群众家中走访慰问，用实际行动帮扶困难群众。四是持续加强人本管理。完善现行人事制度体系，印发《莱商银行员工管理办法》《莱商银行员工岗位交接管理办法》《莱商银行员工及时奖励办法》，提高人事管理水平。强化教育培训，制定年度培训计划，努力提高培训的针对性与实效性，提高员工及管理人员的综合素养。借助外部权威师资力量，举办两期中高层管理人员脱产培训、两期内训师培训、一期后备干部培训，有效提高了中高层管理人员、后备干部的综合素养及内训师工作质量。大力推进暖心工程，建立职工互助基金，坚持把温暖送到员工心中生活中，有效减轻困难职工负担。二、报告期内主要财务会计数据（一）报告期内贷款投放情况1．贷款构成：单位：人民币千元类
